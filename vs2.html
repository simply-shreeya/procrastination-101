About Us

  Welcome to Procrastination 101, the ultimate resource hub for BCA students! 
  We know how overwhelming academic life can get, especially when you’re juggling assignments, projects, exams, and maybe even a 
  little procrastination.
  That’s where we come in. We’ve created this platform to help you stay organized and focused, so you can find everything you need 
  for your studies without the hassle.
 
  On Procrastination 101, you’ll find a comprehensive collection of study materials, previous year question papers (PYQs), 
  assignments, and class presentations (PPTs), all curated specifically for BCA students. Our mission is to make sure that no student
  wastes time searching for resources across different platforms, WhatsApp groups, or class drives. Instead, everything is available
  at your fingertips, in one well-organized space.
        
  Our platform is built by students who understand the challenges you face, from those last-minute 
  assignment submissions to late-night study sessions. We want to make it easier for you to tackle your workload with confidence 
  and ease. Whether you’re catching up on missed lectures, preparing for an exam, or just looking to brush up on a topic,
  Procrastination 101 is here to support you.
  Currently, our focus is on BCA courses, but we have big plans for the future! As we grow, we aim to expand our offerings 
  and cover a wider range of subjects and resources. We believe in turning procrastination into productivity 
  by giving you quick, easy access to the tools you need to succeed.
        
  So, why stress when you can stay on track with Procrastination 101? Join us and take control of your academic journey today!
